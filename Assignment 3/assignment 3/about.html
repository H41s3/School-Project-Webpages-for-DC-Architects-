History

DC Architects emerged onto the architectural scene in 1987, founded by Bruce Wayne. With a vision to redefine architectural standards, David opened the doors of DC Architects in Melbourne. The firm quickly gained recognition for its commitment to excellence and innovative designs. Over the years, DC Architects has become synonymous with iconic structures that stand as a testament to our dedication and creativity. From our modest beginnings to a team of 200 professionals, we have continued to evolve while staying true to our core values. Thank you for considering DC Architects as your architectural partner. We look forward to collaboratively creating spaces that exceed your expectations.
